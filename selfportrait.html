Self-portrait

THE

Video about Evita.

A short movie based on the task to create a portrait about another person. I had to work with the entire production process behind the video, including: idea development and project management. It was my first introduction to the cinematic tools, video equipment and technical terms.

IDEA

Each one of us plays a dirrerent character based on the comfort zone we have with other people. You are a different person when you are with your partner, boss or a stranger. The only person who really knows you is you.

So does Evita. Therefore, there are 3 different characters in the video. Evita as herself, as an actress and as a writer.

The project required that I worked ‘in the field‘. It was rather difficult because the set consisted only of her room that I have seen for the first time when we met for the shooting. I needed to create three different sets that would clearly show that she plays three different personas that fight for the spotlight.

The SOUL set has her photos from her life in the background. Flowers because she loves nature. And a hidden alien photo to indicate that this is her higher-self talking. She is also the one who is naming the things she likes while she is questioning what makes her being her.

Her ACTRESS persona is shown in front of a plain wall but it is desperately trying to merge with the soul persona.

The WRITER is in a dim light because she is hidden in the shadows. But by the end we find out that she is the one controlling the whole narrative.
